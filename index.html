Common Questions

BEFORE YOU APPLY

Do I need experience to apply for The Fellows Table?+

No. The Fellows Table is designed for students and emerging creatives who are early in their journey. We're looking for curiosity, initiative, and a willingness to learn -- not a perfect resume.

How does payment work for The Espresso Club?+

Rates are discussed before each project begins. You'll know exactly what you're earning before you commit. Payment is issued after project completion per your freelance agreement.

Can I be in more than one program at a time?+

Yes. For example, an Espresso Club freelancer could also be a Founders Club member. The programs serve different needs and can complement each other.

What's the difference between the two Founders Club tiers?+

The Founding Seat gives you community access, strategy resources, and regular insights. The Corner Booth adds hands-on support: private 1-on-1 strategy sessions, monthly office hours, service discounts, and priority booking.

Is the Founders Club a replacement for hiring Sunday & Co.?+

No. The Founders Club gives you community, resources, and strategic guidance. If you need full-service marketing support, that's a separate engagement. Corner Booth members get 10% off and priority booking when they're ready.

How long is the Fellows Table internship?+

Each cohort runs for one season -- summer or fall. The time commitment is 5-10 hours per week with flexible scheduling around your classes or other obligations.

I submitted my application. What happens next?+

We review every application personally. You'll hear back within two weeks at the email you provided. For Founders Club applicants, you'll receive a checkout link to complete your membership.

Can I cancel my Founders Club membership?+

Yes. Monthly memberships can be cancelled anytime. Annual memberships are non-refundable but you keep full access through the end of your term.
